Step 1 of 3
Turn on Google's switch
Google requires one switch (the “Apps Script API”) to be ON before any app may build things in your account. Takes 10 seconds — you only ever do this once.
set up manually instead
Step 2 of 3
Sign in with Google
Prizm signs you in, then creates your Sheet and its engine automatically — all inside your own Google Drive. Your data never leaves your account.
Step 3 of 3
Tap “Allow” on the Google tab
A Google tab opens asking for a one-time OK to run your new engine. Choose your account, tap Allow, then come back here — Prizm connects by itself. This can take a few seconds.
